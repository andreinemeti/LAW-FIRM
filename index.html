About Us

We are a leading business law firm serving companies and large corporations since 1991. At ABC, we work in collaboration, having 2 offices in Romania, 1 in Europe and 1 in the USA. Our main headquarter is situated in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. The key to our succes has been our ability to understand the needs of our long-established network of clients and partners. We were ranked first place as the most trusted law firm in Cluj-Napoca
